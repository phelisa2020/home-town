Top three activities to do at Torshov

visit the Iziko South African Museum

Check out beautifully preserved plant and animal specimens, as well as wonderfully preserved fossils and tools developed by early hominids.

Take a day trip to Table Mountain

Towering over the city, it has long been an icon for a reason. No matter what adventure level you prefer, it’s one of the most fun things to do in Cape Town.

Take some wine tours

South Africa and wine are truly an iconic pairing. On the aptly named Winelands tour, you’ll spend the day exploring such famous areas as Franschhoek and Stellenbosch, often in the company of wine experts.
